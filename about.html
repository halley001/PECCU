Our History

With the help of the Cameroon Co-operative Credit Union League Limited (CamCCUL,) the Pecten Employees Cooperative Credit Union (PECCU) came into being on February 28, 1986 with 39 members signing up. PECCU immediately affiliated itself to CamCCUL, purely because it was the right thing to do, and also because it needed to be affiliated to a trust worthy institution.

A Board of Directors, made up of five people was elected to run the affairs of the Union, and most strategic decisions were brought up to the board to be handled.

Right after the initiation of this credit union, Pecten Cameroon Company granted the Union Payroll deduction facilities and as soon as it was set up, membership grew from 39 to 150 within a short time while total savings grew to 117 million CFA.

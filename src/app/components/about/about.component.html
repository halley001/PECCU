About Us

OPSECS Ltd is a Category II MFE created on 18 December 2013. It started as a Category I Financial Cooperative Society named Opus Securitatis Savings and Loan Cooperative Society Limited (OPSEC) that opened its doors to the public on the 22nd of January, 2014.

It was meant to be a Category I Microfinance Institution akin to a Credit Union under law No 92/006 of August 14th 1992 and its Decree of implementation 92/455/PM of November 23 1992. In addition to its registration, given the activities undertaken, OPSEC also came under the terms of Decree No 98/300/PM of September 9 1988, defining the terms of operations of credit and savings cooperatives.

In a quest to offer good services to its clients, during the Board of Directors meeting of 3rd April 2014 with a majority of shareholders present, it was resolved that the institution be transformed from a Category I MFI to a Category II MFI and its name changed from Opus Securitatis Savings and Loan Cooperative Society Limited to Opus Securitatis Solidarity Limited (OPSECS Ltd).

Opus Securitatis Solidarity Limited

OPSECS Ltd is a Category II microfinance created to facilitate and promote the financial sustainability of its customers.

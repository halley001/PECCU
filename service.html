My Account

We offer the following services

Ordinary Shares Account.

This type of share qualifies you a joint owner of PECCU along with other members, and gives you the right to vote and to be voted as a Board member or committee member of PECCU at the Annual General Meeting. These shares yield interest but jointly calculated with interest on savings payable at the end of each year.

VARIABLE SHARES

This type of share is same as Ordinary shares, but available to members who would like to increase their shareholding in PECCU.

REDEEMABLE PREFERENCE SHARES

This type of share yields interest, one RPS is 1,500,000FCFA. Maturity period for RPS investment is 10 years minimum with fixed interest rate of 3% per annum.

For a member to be considered active and regular in PECCU he or she must save at least 25,000FCFA per month. Prompt cash Withdrawals from savings accounts attract a charge of 5%, except a member gives a notice to withdraw from savings.
